About Mytri Engineering Services

Professional facility management and engineering service provider

Company Overview

Mytri Engineering Services is a specialized utility, HVAC, and facility-management service firm operating from Prahaladhapuram, Visakhapatnam, serving industrial and commercial clients such as pharma plants, offices, hospitals, and apartments.

With innovative and forward-thinking methods, Mytri ensures smooth and efficient functioning of industrial utilities, HVAC systems, and infrastructure facilities across various industries.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable one-stop solution for all engineering and facility management requirements through advanced technologies, dynamic service teams, and customer-first support.

Our Journey

Early Years

Company founded in Visakhapatnam as "Mytri Engineering Services," focusing on operation and maintenance of boilers, chilling plants, and HVAC utilities for local industries.

Expansion into Utilities and Facilities

Services expanded from boilers to full HVAC and refrigeration utilities, including VAM chillers, compressors, and industrial refrigeration systems.

Facility Management Focus

Facility-management offerings were added: housekeeping, routine plant upkeep, manpower supply, and general electrical and mechanical maintenance.

Recent Operations

Today the company continues to operate from Prahaladhapuram, Visakhapatnam, providing a combined package of HVAC, boiler, water-treatment, and facility-management services.
